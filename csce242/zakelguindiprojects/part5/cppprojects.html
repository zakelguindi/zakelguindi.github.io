Locker Doors Algorithm

August 2023

I was tasked to write a c++ algorithm that would solve the following puzzle: There are n lockers in a hallway, numbered sequentially 1 through n. Initially, all the locker doors are closed. You make r passes by the lockers, each time starting with locker #1. On the i-th pass, i=1, 2, ..., r, you toggle the door of every other i-th locker. If the door is closed, you open it, if it's opened, you close it. After the last pass display:
1) The locker door # and their status (opened/closed)
2) How many locker doors are open?
